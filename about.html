About Solapur Chaddur

Our Heritage

Solapur, located in Maharashtra, India, has been a center of textile weaving for centuries. The Solapur Chaddur (bedsheet) is an iconic product that represents the culmination of traditional weaving techniques and artistic excellence.

The Craft

Our chaddurs are woven on traditional wooden looms using time-honored methods. Each piece takes considerable skill and patience to create. The weaving process involves:

  • Yarn Preparation: Fine cotton is carefully selected and prepared
  • Loom Setup: Traditional wooden looms are meticulously arranged
  • Hand Weaving: Master weavers skillfully interlace threads
  • Finishing: Final touches ensure quality and perfection

Our Mission

We are committed to:

  • Preserving centuries-old weaving traditions
  • Supporting artisan communities and fair trade practices
  • Delivering authentic, high-quality products
  • Promoting sustainable and eco-friendly practices

Quality Standards

Every Solapur Chaddur undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards. We use only natural materials and traditional techniques to create products that are not just beautiful, but also durable and long-lasting.
